## 🎵 Music and/or Podcasts

Today, my recommendation is the album Back to Black (2006), by Amy Winehouse.

Her second album, Back to Black, has a strong influence of Soul and R&B, in addition to great songs. It manages to be sensitive and dancing at the same time.  
This is one of those albums you can't find anything to say bad about.

There's no way you can listen to the album and not be delighted, even with sad songs, like 'Rehab'.

- [Back to Black by Amy Winehouse](https://music.apple.com/us/album/back-to-black/1422677780)
