This blog is part of the open web. It uses open, accessible standards to enable you to follow along with my updates using whichever method you prefer. Pick your favorite method below - or as many as you like!

### **Email**

Get updates directly in your inbox. Just click the subscribe button below. I'll never use this email address for anything else.

### **RSS**

This lets you use a feed reader such as [Miniflux](https://miniflux.app/) to keep up with my latest posts. You can insert my blog's URL into your favorite reader, or use the direct link:

[https://afranca.com.br/rss/](/rss/)

### **ActivityPub**

If you're on the decentralized social network known as the fediverse, using popular software such as Mastodon, Misskey, Gotosocial, or Pleroma, you can follow this blog to get my updates directly in your social stream. Just search for my handle and click “Follow”.

**@andre@afranca.com.br**
